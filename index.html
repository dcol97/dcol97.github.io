About Me:

I am currently a postdoc at Purdue University and Georgia Tech working with Vassilis Zikas and Juan Garay and have been since June 2024.
I obtained a PhD from EPFL, Switzerland in cryptography under the supervision of Serge Vaudenay in April 2024; I worked at EPFL from September 2019 to April 2024.
Before this, I studied Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Sydney, where I spent time under the supervision of Vincent Gramoli in distributed computing which culminated in an Honours thesis.
So far, I work on or have worked on the following topics:

  • Secure messaging and ratcheting, in both the two party and group settings
  • Distributed computing (consensus and beyond)
  • Side channels
  • Symmetric cryptography
Please feel free to get in touch if you would like to chat or collaborate.
